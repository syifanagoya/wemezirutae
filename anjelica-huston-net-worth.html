What was Huston’s early life like?

Yes, her first significant role was in her father’s film A Walk with Love and Death. Their professional relationship was complex and had a profound impact on her early career.

What roles did she play on television?

Has she written any books?

Yes, she has authored memoirs titled A Story Lately Told and Watch Me, where she discusses her life and career.

What is Huston’s connection to the fashion world?

Before becoming a renowned actress, Huston was a fashion model, working with prominent photographers and appearing in major fashion magazines during the 1960s and 70s.

How did Huston’s mother, Enrica Soma, influence her career?

Her mother’s tragic death deeply affected her, leading Huston to relocate to New York and transition from modeling to acting.
