Who Are Stanley Sandler And Judy Sandler? Meet Adam Sandler Parents

After graduating from high school, a young Sandler was inspired to pursue a career in show business and enrolled in lessons at the ‘Lee Strasberg Theater & Film School’ in New York City.

Adam Sandler’s first television appearance was in the late 1980s on ‘The Cosby Show,’ when he played a friend of the Huxtable family. 

He has appeared frequently on the ‘MTV’ game show ‘Remote Control.’ At this time, he continued to do stand-up comedy and gained the notice of comic Dennis Miller, who suggested him to the ‘Saturday Night Live team (SNL).
