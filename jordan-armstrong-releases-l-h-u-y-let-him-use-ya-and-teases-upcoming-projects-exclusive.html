JorDan Armstrong Releases L.H.U.Y. (Let Him Use Ya) and Teases Upcoming Projects (Exclusive

 Posted on June 9, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  712 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Louisiana born singer-songwriter, producer, and actor, Jor’Dan Armstrong recently released his new single “L.H.U.Y. (Let Him Use Ya),” and Celeb Secrets had the opportunity chat with the artist and hear all about the new track as well as upcoming projects.

Armstrong shared that his inspiration behind “L.H.U.Y. (Let Him Use Ya)” goes back to a saying he heard a lot during his childhood and remembers even now.

“’L.H.U.Y. (Let Him Use Ya),’ is a fun song that plays on a slogan I would hear in church as a kid,” he tells Celeb Secrets. “We would constantly yell or shout ‘Let Him Use Ya’ in church to encourage whoever was speaking or singing.”

We sat down with Jor’Dan (virtually) to talk all about “L.H.U.Y. (Let Him Use Ya),” and the creative process behind the track, his musical inspirations, and what’s to come in his new music.

You can also connect with Jor’Dan by giving him a follow on Instagram at @wheresjor_dan.

Celeb Secrets: Can you introduce yourself and tell us a little bit about yourself for our readers who may just be discovering you?

Jor’Dan Armstrong (JA): “I’m a singer-songwriter, producer, and actor originally from Louisiana.”

CS: When did you start singing and what inspired you to get into music? When did you decide to pursue singing professionally?

JA: “I started singing at the young age of five. My mom made me sing my first solo in church and to be honest I enjoyed the response from the congregation and honestly, I think that is what started my music career lol. I started doing music professionally at the age of 18.”

CS: Who are some artists that inspire your music/artistry?

JA: “So many artists inspire me; like the late great Michael Jackson and Prince. But new artists such as Drake and Kanye West also really inspire me.”

CS: Your recent release “L.H.U.Y. (Let Him Use Ya)” is so good! For people that haven’t heard it, what can they expect from it?

JA: “‘L.H.U.Y. (Let Him Use Ya)’ is such a fun song that plays on a slogan I would hear in church as a kid. Ee would constantly yell or shout ‘Let Him Use Ya’ in church to encourage whoever was speaking or singing.”

CS: Can you go more in-depth about the meaning behind the song and its creative process?

JA: “The creative process of the song was easy as far as the music, it was a track I produced in about an hour and the song’s lyrics just began to flow. I played it for my team and they loved it, at that moment I knew I had something.”

CS: You’ve teased that your upcoming album is coming sometime in 2022 — how has the process been going? What can you tease from this upcoming music?

JA: “2022 is going to be amazing! I’m excited about the new music and I have some special guest features. I think when you guys hear who they are, you’ll be excited as well.”

CS: Will it have a similar sound to “L.U.H.Y (Let Him Use Ya)”?

JA: “The sound of this project is very mixed so be on the lookout for that!”

CS: If you could describe your upcoming album/music in three words, what would they be and why?

JA: “Fun, inspirational, and uplifting, each of those are so necessary in these times.”

CS: All your songs are so open, honest and just all around inspirational. When you make music, what’s the main thing that you want listeners to take from your songs?

JA: “Vulnerability! I really believe people can hear it through the music and they can feel it.”

CS: Lastly, since we are Celeb Secrets, what are some behind-the-scenes secrets from making your music that people wouldn’t know just by listening to it?

JA: “That this project is being recorded and created in the guest bedroom of my condo (laughs).”
