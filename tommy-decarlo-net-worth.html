Tommy DeCarlo Finance Overview

Early Life and Musical Beginnings

Tommy DeCarlo was born on April 23, 1965, in Utica, New York. From a young age, he was passionate about music, particularly rock.

His early years were marked by an admiration for the band Boston, which significantly influenced his musical style.

Despite his love for music, DeCarlo initially pursued a career outside the industry, working as a credit manager at a Home Depot in Charlotte, North Carolina.

Journey to Becoming Boston’s Lead Singer

In 2007, the tragic passing of Boston‘s original lead singer, Brad Delp, created a void in the band. DeCarlo, a dedicated fan, paid tribute to Delp by recording a song and posting it on MySpace.

His heartfelt tribute and covers of Boston’s songs caught the attention of Tom Scholz, Boston’s founding member. This unexpected discovery led to an invitation to perform at the Brad Delp Tribute Concert.

His performance was well-received, leading to his permanent role as Boston’s lead vocalist in 2008.

Career with Boston

Since joining Boston, DeCarlo has been an integral part of the band. He has participated in several major tours, including those in 2008, 2012, 2014, 2015, 2016, and 2017.

His contributions to the band’s 2013 album, Life, Love & Hope, include lead vocals on several tracks such as Life, Love & Hope, Someday, You Gave Up on Love (2.0), and The Way You Look Tonight.

These performances have solidified his place in the band’s legacy and contributed significantly to his financial success.

Formation and Success of DeCarlo Band

In addition to his role with Boston, Tommy DeCarlo has pursued his musical aspirations with his own band, DeCarlo, formed in 2012 with his son, Tommy DeCarlo Jr. The band signed with Frontier Records and released their debut album, Lightning Strikes Twice, in 2020.

The album and its singles, such as There She Goes and A Better Day, received positive reviews and further established DeCarlo’s presence in the rock music scene.

Their latest album, Dancing In The Moonlight, released in 2022, continues to showcase their musical talents and expand their fan base.
