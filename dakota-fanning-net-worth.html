Dakota Fanning is an American actress known for her rich filmography from a very young age. She was born on February 23, 1994, in Conyers, Georgia, USA.

What was Dakota Fanning’s breakthrough role?

Her breakthrough role was as Lucy Dawson in the film I Am Sam (2001), for which she became the youngest nominee in Screen Actors Guild Award history at just seven years old.

What are some notable films Dakota Fanning has appeared in?

She has starred in various films including War of the Worlds, The Twilight Saga, The Runaways, Coraline, and Once Upon a Time in Hollywood.

Has Dakota Fanning won any awards?
